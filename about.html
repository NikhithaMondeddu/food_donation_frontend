About Us

Welcome to our Food Donation Platform. We are a student-led initiative dedicated to reducing food waste and helping communities in need. Our platform allows individuals and organizations to donate surplus food safely and conveniently.

Every contribution makes a difference. By connecting donors with volunteers and food banks, we aim to ensure that no good food goes to waste. Thank you for being a part of our mission.

Together, we can build a more sustainable and caring world.
